## Analysis

The PPO checkpoint at 5,146 games shows meaningful improvement over the 4k eval:
- Average floor climbed from 14.70 to 15.44, now within 0.34 floors of the heuristic (15.78)
- Best single run hit floor 42 (Act 3), the deepest any policy has reached
- Elite win rate improved from 74.8% to 79.9%, approaching the heuristic's 81.9%

The Act 1 boss remains the primary bottleneck. The agent reaches the boss in ~62.5% of runs but only converts ~31% of those into victories, dying on floor 16 in roughly 42% of all runs. The heuristic converts at 39%, suggesting the model has room to improve fight decision-making (card play sequencing, potion usage, card selection for boss matchups).

Entropy settled into the 0.25-0.29 band during this training segment, and KL stayed well below the 0.03 target, indicating stable policy updates. The auto-tune system maintained healthy dynamics without intervention.

## Next Steps

- Analyze floor-16 deaths to identify specific boss matchup weaknesses
- Consider targeted reward shaping for Act 1 boss fights
- Run longer training (target 8-10k games) to see if the current trajectory continues closing the heuristic gap
- Investigate whether the model is learning boss-specific strategies or still relying on general play patterns
